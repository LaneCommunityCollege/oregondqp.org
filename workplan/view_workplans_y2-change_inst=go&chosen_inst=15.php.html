Year 2 - Horizontal Alignment (Tillamook Bay Community College)

Institutional Objectives - Outcome to be achieved

A: Participate in monthly conference calls to share information and get updates about the work.

B: Send representatives to the DQP Fall Conference on October 25, 2013 in Salem.

C: Send representatives to the Oregon-ized Summit on January 10, 2014 in Portland.

Linkages - Related institutional initiatives/projects

A: Campus awareness of DQP; learning outcomes and assessment work (i.e., student learning outcomes assessment).

B: Work on learning outcomes; general education outcomes and program review; and student learning outcomes.

C: Work on student progression and completion; knowledge of higher education environment, research, and best practices.

Processes - How outcome will be achieved

A: At least one DQP lead (andministrative and/or faculty) will participate in monthly Oregon DQP calls.

B: DQP leads (administrative and faculty) and at least one General Education faculty member will register for and attend Oregon DQP Fall Conference.

C: One or more TBCC representatives will register for and attend the Oregon-ized Summit (winter) in Portland on January 10, 2014.
