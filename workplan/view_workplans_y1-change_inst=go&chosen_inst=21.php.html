Year 1 - Vertical Integration (Portland State University)

Institutional Objectives - Outcome to be achieved

A: Institutional Objectives - Outcome to be achieved

A. Pilot assessment of sample of student's culminating projects using VALUE/DQP framework in four contexts, and in each context engage external partners from CCs:
1. University Studies (The Junior year and articulation inflection point)
2. SBA representing the comparison of DQP with professional accreditation outcomes
3. PA representing engagement and in context of high impact practice
4. GSE Administrative Licensure Program representing the BA/Graduate vertices and the opportunity to sound DQP with professional K-12 leaders.

B:

C:

Linkages - Related institutional initiatives/projects

A: Linkages - Related institutional initiatives/projects
B. The work reflects a year of work in online outcomes-based backwards design, a collaborative curriculum development process developed for coherence and efficiencies

B:

C:

Processes - How outcome will be achieved

A: Processes - How outcome will be achieved
A. The design process engaged faculty from four disciplines in the design. The work was initiated with the end in mind (Backwards design), and the VALUE rubrics were used anticipating the DQP potential and opportunity. In that sense, the workplan is embedded in the COL design process. The work will be analyzed and presented at the AAC&U meeting in Atlanta, as far as it is ready at that time.

C:

Progression - Milestones and dates to achievement

A: Progression - Milestones and dates to achievement
A. The year one milestone will be assessed at the completion of the winter term and again in early January as work assessment is completed. It is likely that not all four programs will have the requisite work to report in early January.
B. Additional milestones will be determined after the anticipated reorganization PSU will implement in January, 2013.
