Institutional Work Plans

Broadly stated, the primary purpose of the Oregon DQP is to engage institutional constituencies in purposeful discussion and reflection on the meaning of their degrees. More specifically, the project is designed to use the DQP concept to create a flexible non-prescriptive curricular framework of essential meta learning outcomes that characterizes, and where possible aligns, overarching degree-level learning outcomes within and among Oregonâ€™s two-year and four-year institutions.
