May 17, 2013 Video Conference

View Full Length Video of this Video Conference

Statewide DQP Spring Video Conference

   05/17/2013 → 05/17/2013

Noon-2:30 p.m.

The Oregon Degree Qualifications Profile project is one of many collaborative efforts among Oregon Community Colleges and Oregon University System with a goal to strengthen student learning and success in Oregon post-secondary institutions. Our May 17 video conference will provide a review of the work we have accomplished over the past year, a synopsis of lessoned learned by those working closely with the DQP, and a summary of next year’s goals and plans. Ron Baker and Carol Schaafsma, co-coordinators of the DQP project in Oregon, will be joined by Marcus Kolb, Strategy Officer at the Lumina Foundation, and representatives from several institutions who have been working with the DQP in Oregon.
